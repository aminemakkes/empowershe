About Our Organization

At EmpowerShe , we are dedicated to SDG 1: No Poverty. Where we empower breast cancer survivors, ensuring they never have to face poverty. Through skills training and employment opportunities, we provide financial independence, aligning with SDG 1's vision of a poverty-free world for these resilient women .

Who are EmpowerShe

We empowers women with breast cancer to create and sell products like jewelry, tote bags, and cushions by providing materials and support. Each individuals has some unique skills so we need to observe and treat each as a special case and develop it so that we can build a new aspect of project to create a source of job that doesn’t requires too much physical touch but easy and not stressful. .
